Privacy Policy

Progress Tracker App

Last Updated: October 2024

Overview

Progress Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting the privacy of parents and caregivers who use our app to track their child's developmental progress. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Voice Recordings: Audio recordings of progress updates
  • Transcriptions: Text converted from voice recordings
  • Photos: Images of documents, activities, or milestones
  • Documents: Scanned documents and reports
  • Progress Notes: Text updates about your child's development
  • Categories & Tags: Organizational data you create

Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: How you interact with app features
  • Device Information: Device type and iOS version for compatibility
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous technical data to improve app stability

How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Track and visualize your child's developmental progress
  • Generate AI-powered insights and progress reports
  • Provide personalized recommendations
  • Sync data across your devices
  • Improve app functionality

Data Storage & Security

Where Data is Stored

  • Cloud Storage: Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) in Mumbai region (asia-south1)
  • Local Storage: On your device for offline access
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest

Data Retention

  • Your data remains in your account until you delete it
  • Deleted data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • You can export all your data at any time

AI Processing

We use Google's Gemini AI to:

  • Analyze progress patterns
  • Generate insights and summaries
  • Answer questions about your child's development
  • Categorize updates automatically
Important: AI processing is done securely and your data is never used to train AI models.

Data Sharing

We NEVER:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Use your child's data for marketing
  • Allow unauthorized access to your information

We ONLY share data when:

  • You explicitly request it (export/sharing features)
  • Required by law (with valid legal process)
  • Necessary to protect safety and security

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Export: Download your data in readable formats
  • Delete: Remove your account and all associated data
  • Modify: Edit or correct any information
  • Control: Choose what features to use

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for parents/caregivers to track children's progress. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children under 13. All data should be entered by adult users only.

Account Security

To protect your account:

  • Use a strong password
  • Enable two-factor authentication when available
  • Don't share your login credentials
  • Sign out on shared devices

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.
